About
CELEBRATING
40 YEARS OF
LPG EXPERTISE
Jayagas takes great pleasure in being your one-stop destination for all your LPG gas and servicing needs. Our firm has been around since 1982, when we distributing bulk LPG and petroleum supplies to commercial, industrial, and domestic customers throughout Malaysia, but we've since branched out to offer engineering and maintenance service as well.
In addition to focusing on what our customers want, we're also serious about adhering to regulations set out by the Malaysia Energy Commission and the Department of Occupational Safety and Health. In other words, you can count on us to supply you with safe and trustworthy LPG gas goods and services for any and all of your requirements.
